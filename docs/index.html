Holter Notes / Holter 笔记

"Holter Notes" is a minimalist note-taking application designed specifically for Holter wearers.

Unlike other note-taking applications, "Holter Notes" focuses on simplifying the startup and recording process to the greatest extent possible, making it easier for Holter wearers to quickly record the exact time of an uncomfortable, cold environment, exercise, etc., providing additional information for diagnosis.

Note: Your data won't leave your phone, so please export your data in a timely manner.

"Holter 笔记" 是专门为动态心电图仪 (Holter) 佩戴者设计的极简笔记应用。

与其他笔记应用不同，"Holter 笔记" 专注于尽最大可能简化启动和记录流程，方便动态心电图仪 (Holter) 佩戴者快速记录身体不适、寒冷环境、运动等事件的准确时间，为诊断提供辅助信息。

注意：您的数据不会离开您的手机，因此请及时导出数据。
